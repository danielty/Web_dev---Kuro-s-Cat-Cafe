"Every pet is a good pet." -- Kuro's inspiration

Kuro's Cat Café is a place cats and people may enjoy each other's company.

The Japanese word "kuro" is used to describe that which is "dark."

Think of Kuro's as a refuge from those "kuro-like" days a towards brighter tomorrow.

For lunch or dinner, we believe food is always tastier in the company of good cats.
